Publications

IOT Based Smart Beehive Monitoring System

IEEE 6th International Conference on Advancements in Computing (ICAC) • 2024

This paper presents a novel IoT-based framework for real-time beehive monitoring tailored to the Sri Lankan beekeeping industry. By integrating environmental sensors and a web-based interface, the system enhances hive management by enabling remote tracking of temperature, humidity, weight, and feeding needs.

Initial results demonstrate significant potential to reduce manual labor and improve hive productivity, laying the foundation for scalable smart agriculture solutions.
